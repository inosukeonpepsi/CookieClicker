    Cookie Clicker: A Simple Game With Endless Depths

    Cookie Clicker starts simple: you click a giant cookie to earn more cookies. But those cookies can buy you tools, like cursors and grandmas, to generate even more cookies automatically. It spirals into a complex management game with bizarre upgrades and achievements.

    Despite its silly premise, Cookie Clicker offers surprising depth. You strategize purchases, unlock secret milk, and chase achievements that push the game's boundaries. It's a captivating idle game, perfect for casual play with a surprising amount of content to devour.
